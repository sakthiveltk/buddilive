Creating a new Buddi Live account

Creating an account is easy:

  1. Go to the main login page
  2. Click the "Register" tab
  3. Enter your email address, choose a locale and currency, and verify that you have read and agree with the terms and conditions of use.
  4. Click 'Generate Key' to send an activation key to the email address you have specified.

  5. Check your email for the registration email. It will contain a key, which looks something like '3ea2d837-b8fa-4684-be1a-c17eeaff39fc'.
  6. Copy / paste the key into the 'Activation Key' field
  7. Choose a password

  8. Click 'Create Account'
At this point, you will be directed back to the login page. Enter your email address and new password, and you will be logged in.
